Latency Budgets Are Product Decisions

February 27, 2026 · Performance
Posts Books about

Engineers like to chase milliseconds. Product teams like to chase trust. The bridge is a latency budget.

Pick a budget from the experience you want: fast enough to feel direct, slow enough to be honest about reality. Then carve it into pieces. If the backend gets 80 ms, the database gets 30, and the network gets 20, you now know exactly where to look.

No graphs required to start. Write the budget in the README, ship one measurement, and hold the line.

When you break the line, you are making a product tradeoff. Say it out loud.
